Cake Mix Cookies

Description

These cake mix cookies are yummy and simple to make with just 3 easy ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 (15.25 ounce) package white cake mix
  • Â½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Stir cake mix, oil, and eggs together in a large bowl until it comes together as a dough
  3. Form dough into 1-inch balls and place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven, about 6 to 10 minutes, depending on how soft you like your cookies.
    Cool cookies on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
